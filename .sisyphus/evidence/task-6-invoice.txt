## Implementation Details

### InvoiceService Methods:
1. `createInvoice(params)` - Creates a new invoice via Fiber RPC
2. `getPaymentStatus(paymentHash)` - Checks payment status
3. `parseInvoiceAddress(address)` - Placeholder for invoice parsing
4. `rpcCall<T>(method, params)` - Private RPC helper

### Configuration:
- RPC URL from config, env var FIBER_RPC_URL, or default http://127.0.0.1:8227
- Biscuit token from config or env var FIBER_BISCUIT_TOKEN
- Default expiry: 3600 seconds (1 hour)

### Fiber RPC Methods Used:
- `new_invoice` - Creates new invoice
- `get_payment` - Gets payment status
